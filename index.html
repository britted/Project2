What we're all about


Mortar Board was founded in 1918 with chapters at Cornell University, The University of Michigan, The Ohio State University and Swarthmore College. As an honor society, Mortar Board is an association of individuals selected for distinguished ability and achievement in scholarship, leadership and service - the three ideals of Mortar Board. We all share a common passion for academic excellence and have exemplified these pilars throughout our collegiate careers.

How we stay involved


Mortar Board members have a strong committment to service and spend our senior year working with one another to positively impact the community through tutoring, community service events, and through Young Storytellers - our main event during Mortar Board week.

Mortar Board week is our exciting week to promote the national project "Reading is Leading"! It is a week filled with great events, speakers, and the Young Storytellers Festival. At Young Storytellers Festival, campus improv groups will act out stories written by stuents from Buder Elementary. It's always a great week, so be sure to sign up for our email reminders so you don't miss it next year!
